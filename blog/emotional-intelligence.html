Emotional Intelligence

"What is Emotional Intelligence?"

Emotional Intelligence (or EQ) is one's ability to understand and manage one's own or others emotions. It affects how we think and the actions we take.

"How is it different to IQ?"

IQ (Intelligence Quotient) is a score given based on tests that represent one's intelligence. It often reflects one's ability to problem solve and is often used to better measure one's competency.
While IQ could be considered the basis for one's logic in decision making, EQ would be the basis for making decisions based on emotional understanding.

"Why is it important?"

EQ allows us to better understand ourselves and how to interact with others. Without it we would be liable to make decisions that could harm ourselves or others without knowing how or why.
