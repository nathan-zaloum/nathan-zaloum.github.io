Foundations Learning Plan

"What is my long term goal and/or career pathway?"

I have studied and worked in Game Development and App Development. Now I am looking to bridge into Web Development. I want to have a strong skillset in all three disciplines and pursue a freelance career.

"Describe my own strengths and limitations in relation to learning."

I am very good at practical exercises and really enjoy doing them. I have a harder time with learning theoretical knoweledge as I have a hard time maintaining my attention.

"What skills (non-technical/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?"

I would like to get better at interacting with people online. It feels disconnected and hard to approach others so I hope to develop a more outgoing approach over this course.

"A commitment to how you will manage your workload."

I plan to simply work until my tasks are complete, then decide whether or not to do extra work or relax. I keep my eye on Slack and prepare myself for the weekly facilitator meetings. If the workload starts to get too much then I will focus less on making things look good and instead focus on simply completing the tasks to an acceptable level.

"A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely manner."

I have a friend who is in the previous cohort and we talk about the course regularly. If I need help he is usually available, however, when he is too busy I will either message the facilitator or pose any questions I might have in Slack.

"A description of what you expect from the facilitator team."

I expect the team to know what I should be doing each week and have a basic understanding of the concepts we are learning.

"Do you have any scheduling conflicts?"

I have some personal projects that I like to work on in my spare time but I can simply not do those when things get busier.

Bootcamp Learning Plan

"What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?"

I have already worked as a Game Developer and an App Developer so I am now looking to add Web Development to my skillset. I would love to be able to work as either a freelance developer or work for a company doing any of those professions.

"What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?"

I don't think my strengths and limitations have changed from my Foundations Learning Plan above. Though I do believe I will have a much better time with my limitations when it comes to Bootcamp.

My biggest strength will be my passion for and pre-existing experience with coding. I often code in my free time anyway, so I look forward to doing lots of fun little projects.

For limitations, it is a shame the cohort hasn't had much opportunity for interaction, so I think many of the group activities may be a little hard at first. Also, while I have experience doing talks in front of others I haven't had to do so in a long time so I think the lightning talks will be a challenge to get used to doing again.

"What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?"

From foundations I have learnt that I struggle with communication online. Now that this issue has been made apparent to me I would like to work on improving it. If Bootcamp stays online then I am hoping the increased interactions through lightning talks, group projects, and general increase in workload will help with this. On the other hand, if Bootcamp ends up on campus then it will be much harder for me to work on this issue since I'm confident I have never had communication issues offline.

Also, like I said above, while I have talking experience, I know that public speaking is a skill that can degrade if not practiced regularly. Bootcamp will be my opportunity to refamiliarise myself with that skill.

"What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?"

I expect there to be someone on campus (or available online) 9-5 to be able to ask questions and get help from. I also expect the team as a whole to have competency in the things we will be learning and doing over Bootcamp.

"What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp?"

I expect myself to show up each day and stay the whole day, and sticking around to complete work if needed. I also expect myself to meet deadlines or communicate why I have not. If Bootcamp is online then I expect myself to have the same commitment, but from home instead.

My commitment to how I will manage my workload is to focus on the tasks for each day and focus on completing them to a basic standard first. Only if I have the spare time at the end will I revisit individual tasks to polish them. My focus is completion and understanding over polish.
